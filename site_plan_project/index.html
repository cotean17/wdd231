Site Plan: Maria Jose Photography

Site Name

Maria Jose Photography

This name reflects my identity as a portrait photographer. It’s simple, personal, and easy to remember, which helps with connection and branding.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase my photography work, provide information about the types of sessions I offer (portraits, family, couples, quinceañeras, etc.), list pricing, and give potential clients an easy way to learn about me and contact me.

The goal is to make it easier for people to feel confident booking a session by showing clear, friendly, and professional information.

Scenarios

  • How much do you charge for a family or quinceañera photo session?
  • What kind of photos can I expect from your sessions?
  • Can I see testimonials from other clients?

Color Schema

Purple (#5a2a83): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
Lavender (#e8d8f0): Used for background highlights and section areas.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for main headings to add elegance and personality.
  • Arial: Used for body text and descriptions for a clean and readable look.
